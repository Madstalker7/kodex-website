About Me

I’m a web designer focused on creating clean, modern, and functional websites. I enjoy turning ideas into simple, well-structured digital experiences that not only look good but also work smoothly across devices. I care about clarity, performance, and usability. For me, good design isn’t about adding more — it’s about removing what isn’t needed and letting the essentials shine. Every project I work on is built with intention, from layout and typography to responsiveness and speed. Through KØDEX Visuals, my goal is to help individuals and brands present themselves professionally online with websites that feel modern, reliable, and thoughtfully crafted.

What I Do

As a web designer, I specialize in the following areas:

  • Writing clean, semantic HTML and modern CSS to create structured, accessible, and visually polished websites.

  • Adding interactivity, dynamic behavior, and logic to websites using clean and efficient JavaScript.

  • Designing layouts that adapt perfectly across desktops, tablets, and mobile devices.

  • Creating intuitive interfaces focused on clarity, usability, and user flow.

  • Structuring pages for readability, balance, and strong visual hierarchy.

  • Improving load times, responsiveness, and overall site performance.

  • Ensuring consistent behavior and appearance across all modern browsers.

Future Goals
